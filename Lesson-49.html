Use of This That (Singular)
Use of These Those (Plural)

We use it to refer to people and things, situations and experiences that are close to the speaker or very close in time. We use that/those to refer to people and things, situations and experiences that are more distant, either in time or physically.

This and that are (lesson-49)

Used for singular

This = यह

That = वह

These and those

are used for Plural

These = ये सब

Those= वे सब

For Example :

यह एक किताब है। = This is a book.
वह एक किताब है। = That is a book.

यह एक गुब्बारा है।  = This is a balloon.
वह एक गुब्बारा है। = That is a balloon.

यह एक सेब है। = This is an apple
वह एक सेब है। = That is an apple.

यह  एक  घोड़ा  हैं = This  is  a  horse.
वह एक  घोड़ा  हैं = That  is  a  horse.

ये किताबें है।  = These are books.
वे किताबें हैं।  = Those are books.

ये गुब्बारे हैं।  =These are balloons.
वे गुब्बारे हैं।  =Those are balloons.

ये सेब हैं।  = These are apples.
वे सेब हैं. = Those are apples.
ये घोड़े है।  = These are horses.
वे घोड़े हैं। = Those are horses.

Scrumptious adjective /ˈskrʌmp.ʃəs/ tasting extremely good: 1- scrumptious cakes. 2- A scrumptious breakfast. (lesson-49)

Use of ‘So… that’ & Too….. to’

  • He is very weak.
  • He cannot walk.

We use the pair of a conjunction to fit two sentences logically and grammatically.

  • He is so weak that he cannot walk वह इतना कमजोर है कि चल नहीं सकता|
  • He is too weak to walk वह इतना कमजोर है कि चल नहीं सकता|

Note:- The word “that” is a descriptive conjunction. So it needs a description.

  • He is very poor.
  • He cannot buy clothes.
  • He is so poor that he cannot buy new clothes. वह इतना गरीब है कि नये कपड़े नहीं खरीद सकता।
  • He is too poor to buy new clothes. वह इतना गरीब है कि नये कपड़े नहीं खरीद सकता।

More examples

  • He is too poor to send his kids to school. वह इतना गरीब है कि अपने बच्चों को स्कूल नहीं भेज पाता।
  • He is so poor that he cannot send his kids to school. वह इतना गरीब है कि अपने बच्चों को स्कूल नहीं भेज सकता।
  • The question was too difficult for me to solve. प्रश्न हल करना मेरे लिए बहुत कठिन था |
  • The question was so difficult that I could not solve it. प्रश्न हल करना मेरे लिए बहुत कठिन था|
  • He is too strong for you to beat (him). वह इतना ताकतवर है कि आप उसे हरा नहीं सकते|
  • He is so strong that you cannot beat (him). वह इतना ताकतवर है कि आप उसे हरा नहीं सकते
  • The house is too small to accommodate us. घर हमारे रहने के लिए बहुत छोटा है।
  • The house is so small that it cannot accommodate us. घर हमारे रहने के लिए बहुत छोटा है।
  • He is too stupid to pass the test. वह इतना मुर्ख है की परीक्षा पास नहीं करेगा
  • He is so stupid that he will not pass the test. वह इतना मुर्ख है की परीक्षा पास नहीं करेगा
  • He is too old to walk without support. वह इतना बूढ़ा हो गया है कि बिना सहारे के चल नहीं सकता।
  • He is so old that he cannot walk without support. वह इतना बूढ़ा हो गया है कि बिना सहारे के चल नहीं सकता।
