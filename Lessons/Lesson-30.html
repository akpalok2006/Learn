Vocabulary

Abundance noun (/əˈbʌn.dəns/) आधिक्य, भरमार The situation in which there is more than enough of something: 1-The abundance of money ruins youth. 2- The area has an abundance of wildlife. 2-Wishing you two an abundance of love and happiness. 3-The scholar has an abundance of knowledge. (विद्वान के पास ज्ञान की प्रचुरता होती है।)

Quack noun (/kwæk/) नीम हकीम/ झोला छाप डॉक्टर a person who dishonestly pretends to have medical skills or knowledge: 1-I’ve got a check-up with the quack next week. 2-That quack doesn’t know anything about treating heart disease.

Desperate adj ( /ˈdes.pɚ.ət/) (WANTING) बेताब होना Needing or wanting something very much: 1-I’m desperate for a drink! (मैं एक पेय के लिए बेताब हूँ!) 2-I am not desperate to romance with you. (मैं तुम्हारे साथ रोमांस करने के लिए बेताब नहीं हूं।)

Refuge noun (/ˈref.juːdʒ/) शरण, पनाह, आश्रय लेना (a place that gives) protection or shelter from danger, trouble, unhappiness, etc. 1- They took refuge in a bomb shelter. 2-He took refuge from the storm in a hut. 3- I went to the God refuge (मैं भगवान की शरण में गया).

Dark horse छुपा रुस्तम (idiom) A candidate or competitor about whom little is known but who unexpectedly wins or succeeds. 1- She’s a dark horse. I didn’t know she’d written a novel. 2-Jim is a dark horse in the final.

Appall verb /əˈpɑːl/ To make someone have strong feelings of shock or of disapproval: 1-It is appalling that a child was murdered. 2-I was appalled by what I saw.

Savage adjective /ˈsæv.ɪdʒ/ Extremely violent, wild, or frightening:1- A savage dog/beast. 2- A brutal and savage attack.

Stealthily adverb /ˈstel.θəl.i/ छिपकर (कोई काम करना) Secretly, so that people do not realize what is happening: 1-He stealthily followed his victim.

Use of conjunction “Lest” (कहीं ऐसा न हो कि)

In order to prevent any possibility that something will happen:

If you do something lest something unpleasant should happen, you do it to try to prevent the unpleasant thing from happening.

Structure: – [Sentence + lest + sub + should + verb1st + Ob]

  1. Don’t think about me lest you should fall in love. मेरे बारे में मत सोचो, ऐसा न हो कि तुम प्यार में पड़ जाओ।
  2. Don’t sit there lest you should fall down. वहाँ मत बैठो, ऐसा न हो कि तुम गिर जाओ।
  3. Speak slowly lest he should hear. धीरे बोलो ऐसा न हो कि वह सुन ले।
  4. Work hard lest you should fail. मेहनत करो, कहीं तुम असफल न हो जाओ|
  5. I feared lest I should be late again. मुझे डर था कि कहीं मुझे फिर से देर न हो जाए।
  6. I obeyed her lest she should be angry. मैंने उसकी बात मानी, कहीं ऐसा न हो कि वह क्रोधित हो जाए।
  7. She turned away from the window lest anyone see them. वह खिड़की से दूर चली गई, कहीं ऐसा न हो कि कोई उन्हें देख ले।
  8. He got up early that morning lest he should miss the train. वह उस सुबह जल्दी उठ गया कहीं ऐसा न हो कि ट्रेन छूट जाए।
  9. He paused, afraid lest he says too much. वह रुक गया, डर गया कि कहीं वह बहुत कुछ न कह दे।
  10. Lest anyone should doubt my story, I have brought documents to attest to its truth. कहीं किसी को मेरी कहानी पर शक न हो, मैं इसकी सच्चाई को प्रमाणित करने के लिए दस्तावेज लाया हूं।
  11. Let me put down your telephone number lest I forget it. मुझे आपका टेलीफोन नंबर लिखने दो, कहीं ऐसा न हो कि मैं उसे भूल जाऊं।
