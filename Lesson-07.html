Use of ‘how often’

When asking this question, you are inquiring how often or how frequently someone does a particular thing.

How often do you exercise?
How often do you change your password?
Hi, how often does he meet you?
How often do you listen to your MP3 player?
How often do you need to go to the dentist?
Okay, How often do they attend the class?
How often do you report to your supervisor?
How often do you stretch before working out?
But, How often do you talk to your parents?
How often do you travel?

Jeopardize verb /ˈdʒep.ɚ.daɪz/ to put something in danger of being harmed or damaged: 1- A scandal like this might jeopardize his political career. 2- His foolish behavior may jeopardize his whole future.

  1. Subject को (कोई काम) करने में कितना समय लगता है\ लगा\ लगा था\ लगेगा? This sentence structure helps to express the thought, Subject does something and it takes time in all tenses like the present, past, and future.

How long will it take me to complete the English speaking course? अंग्रेजी बोलने का कोर्स पूरा करने में मुझे कितना समय लगेगा?

How long does it take you to get your father’s point? आपको अपने पिता की बात समझने  में  कितना समय लगता है?

How long did it take Sharma Ji to kill a mosquito? शर्मा जी को एक मच्छर मारने में कितना समय लगा?

How long did it take you to understand that you have to learn English? आपको यह समझने में कितना समय लगा कि आपको अंग्रेजी सीखनी है
