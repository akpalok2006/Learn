Some preposition lesson-48

Busy +V4+obj कोई काम करने में ब्यस्त. The workmen are still busy concreting the road. 2-He was busy assembling the bike by himself.

Stop+VB1+ing+obj कोई काम करना बंद के देना 1-Stop worrying about their marriage-it isn’t your problem. 2-Stop beefing about the price increase. 3-Stop messing about and listen to me. 4-Please stop making that noise?

Instead Of +VB1+ing+obj कोई काम करने के वजाय (किसी व्यक्ति या वस्तु के आलावा). in place of someone or something: You can go instead of me if you want. 2-Focus on small goals instead of big ones. 3-You probably picked up my keys instead of yours.

In spite of +V4+obj कोई काम करने के बावजूद. We use “in spite of” to express a contrast between two things. But one may use “Despite” for formal use. 1-He got the job in spite of his prison record. 2-John’s company is doing extremely well despite the recession. 3-She is cheerful in spite of his illness.

On the pretext of+V4+obj कोई काम करने के बहाने. Pretext noun /(ˈpriː.tekst/) बहाना, a pretended reason for doing something that is used to hide the real reason. 1-I called her on the pretext of needing more information. 2-He used his sore finger as a pretext for not going to school.
