Native short-expression lesson-54

  • What are you going to do?
  • What’re ya going to do?
  • And, what’re ya gonna do?
  • Whatcha gonna do?
  • Similarly, how’re ya doing

Whatcha means “what are you”

  • Wha are you doing? :- Whatcha doing?
  • What are you thinking? :- Whatcha thinking?
  • What are you thinking? :- Whatcha thinking?

Hang on: To wait for a short time: 1-Sally’s on the other phone – would you like to hang on? 2-Do you need the toilet right now or can you hang on for a while? 3- Hang on a minute – I’ll be with you in a moment!
