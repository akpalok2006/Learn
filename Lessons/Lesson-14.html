Used to Grammar

This used to grammar is used to talk about past states as well as past repeated actions and habits

पहचान:- ताथा, तीथी, तेथे (Past habit)

[+] Sub + Used to + VB1 + Object

    [-]  Sub +Did not + Use to + VB1 + Obj

[क्या?]  Did + sub + use to + vb1 + obj

[क्या नहीं?] Didn’t + sub + use to + vb1 + obj

[क्यों?] Why did + sub + use to + vb1 + obj

[क्यों  नहीं?] Why + didn’t + sub + use to vb1 + obj

Vocabulary Lesson-14

  1. Earlier पहले comparative of early
  2. But no longer now पर अब नहीं
  3. Good for nothing noun /ˌɡʊd.fɚˈnʌθ.ɪŋ/ नालायक a person who is lazy and not helpful or useful:
  4. Traitor noun /ˈtreɪ.t̬ɚ/ गद्दार a person who is not loyal or stops being loyal to their own country, social class, beliefs, etc.: 1-The leaders of the rebellion were hanged as traitors.
  5. Industrious adjective /ɪnˈdʌs.tri.əs/ मेहनती An industrious person works hard:
  6. Docile adjective /ˈdɑː.səl/ सीधा -सिदा quiet and easy to influence, persuade, or control:
  7. Scold verb /skoʊld/ डाटना to speak to someone angrily because you disapprove of their behavior:
  8. Consider somebody something किसी को किसी तरह का समझना
  9. Show hospitality to somebody किसी की खातिरदारी
  10. Before + vb1 + ing + obj कोई काम करने से पहले
  11. But Somebody turned out to be… पर कोई किसी तरह का निकलना
  12. Pamper verb /ˈpæm.pɚ/ to treat with too much kindness and attention: बहुत लाड़ प्यार करना

Sentences

  1. Earlier, people used to show hospitality to a guest, but no longer now. पहले लोग मेहमानो की खातिरदारी किया करते थे, पर अब नहीं|
  2. Could Ram have shown hospitality to the guest? क्या राम मेहमानों की खातिरदारी कर सकता था?
  3. Should I have spoken the truth? क्या मुझे सच बोलना चाहिए था?
  4. I used to consider my brother industrious, but he turned out to be good for nothing. मैं अपने भाई को मेहनती मानता था, लेकिन नालायक निकला।
  5. Can you people show hospitality to the guest? क्या आपलोग मेहमानो की खातिरदारी कर सकते है?
  6. Did you use to go to school in childhood? क्या तुम बचपन में स्कूल जाया करते थे?
  7. When all could your father have taken you to the task? तुम्हारे फादर तुम्हारा खबर कब-कब ले सकते थे?
  8. When all should your father take you to the task? तुम्हारे फादर को तुम्हारा खबर कब कब लेना चाहिए? (Lesson-14)
