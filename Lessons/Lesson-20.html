Use of capable and able

Capable:- after the adjective capable, we use the preposition “of” and the verb 4: I am capable of doing this. He is capable of finishing the task on time.

Able:- after the adjective able, we use the infinitive (to + verb):- I am able to do this. He is able to finish the job on time.

Present continuous /Present Progressive/Tense

The present progressive is used to indicate the ongoing time (now). However, the stative verbs do not usually take the form of present progressive even though they refer to the present time
