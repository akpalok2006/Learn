Vocabulary

Harness verb /ˈˈhɑːr.nəs/ To harness something means to control it and use it. 1-harness energy/ideas/skills Organizations need to harness the skills and knowledge of people who are retired or unemployed. 2-harness the power of something: Plans to help developing countries harness the power of technology are important in the fight against global warming.

Fabulous adj /ˈfæb.jə.ləs/ very good; excellent: 1-She looked absolutely fabulous in her dress. 2-They’ve got a fabulous apartment in the center of Paris. 3-We had a fabulous time at the party.

Drift verb, noun /drɪft/ to move slowly, especially as a result of outside forces, with no control over direction: 1-No one noticed that the boat had begun to drift out to sea. 2- People began to drift back to their houses. To understand the general meaning of what someone is saying: 1-I don’t get your drift. 2-So what’s the drift of the argument?

Drift around If you say that someone drifts around, you mean that they travel from place to place without a plan: 1- If you drift around, you will get nowhere.

Carve verb /kɑːrv/ To make something by cutting into especially wood or stone, or to cut into the surface of stone, wood, etc.: 1-This totem pole is carved from/out of a single tree trunk. 2- He carved her name on a tree. To cut thin pieces from a large piece of cooked meat: 1- Would you like me to carve (the chicken)?

Future Continuous Tense Lesson-25

The future continuous tense is used when an action is promised/thought to be going on at a specific time/context in the future.

पहचान: रहा होगा,रही होगी, रहे होंगे, (In future) (Lesson-25)

हम इस कठिन समस्या से निपट रहे होंगे। We will be tackling this difficult problem.

आप शहर में रह रहे होंगे। You will be dwelling in the city.

वह पाठ याद कर रहा होगा । He will be learning the lesson.

तुम अपने नौकर को बुला रहे होगे । You will be calling your servant.

हम तुम्हारे घर आ रहे होंगे । We shall be coming to your house.

मैं दिल्ली नहीं जा रहा होगा । I shall not be going to Delhi.

वह अपनी गुड़िया के साथ नहीं खेल रही होगी । She will not be playing with her doll.

गाय घास नहीं चर रही होगी । The cow will not be grazing grass.
