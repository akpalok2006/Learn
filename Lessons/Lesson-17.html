Rule:- Sub+Must have/ must not have+VB3+Obj

  1. You must have gone abroad. आप विदेश गए होंगे।
  2. She must have seen the Taj Mahal. आपने ताजमहल देखा होगा।
  3. You must have learned the homework. आपने होमवर्क सीख लिया होगा।
  4. He must have apologized. आपने माफी मांगी होगी।
  5. You must have seen that incident. आपने उस घटना को देखा होगा
  6. He must have bluffed to his boss. Hence, his boss fired him from the job. उसने अपने बॉस को झांसा दिया होगा। इसलिए उसके मालिक ने उसे नौकरी से निकाल दिया।
  7. He must have defied to his boss. Hence, his boss fired him from the job. उसने अपने मालिक की अवहेलना की होगी। इसलिए उसके मालिक ने उसे नौकरी से निकाल दिया। (Lesson-17)

Sub+want+SB+to+v1

I want you to’ is telling someone that you have a desire or would like for them to do something.

  • He wants you to call once you get there.
  • I want you to come home right after school.
  • I want you to clean the dishes.
  • My manager wants me to explain myself to him.
  • I want you to educate me.
  • All parents want their children to stand first in the class.
  • Teachers do not want students to linger after the class got over.
  • Parents do not want their children to go astray.
  • The company does not want its employees to make the customer unhappy.
  • The manager does not want flaws in the final reports.
  • Why does your team leader not want you to leave the company?
  • Why don’t all women want their husbands to put on weight?
  • I don’t want you to believe in hearsay.
  • Why doesn’t the company want its customers to lose credibility?
  • The manager does not want us to fabricate an excuse.
  • The technical lead does not want this router to be the root of the trouble.
  • I do not want the rain to start now.
  • His team leader wants him to correct the error right away.
  • I want you to go over this report.
  • She wants me to not mind if she comes late.
