Use of Let Us / Let’s

‘Let us’ is used as a polite way of making or responding to a suggestion, giving an instruction, making an offer of help. (lesson-64)

You say let’s or, in formal English, let us, when you are making a suggestion that involves both you and the person you are talking to, or when you are agreeing to a suggestion of this kind.

‘Let us’ के प्रयोग में ‘आओ हम ….. करें‘ वाले वाक्य होते है । नम्रता से सुझाव देना या जवाब देना, या निर्देश देना या सहायता की पेशकश करना आदि के रूप में प्रयोग होता है 

Rule  Let + Us + Vb1+Object.

Let us / Let’s serve the country. आओ हम देश की सेवा करें ।

आओ हम पिक्चर इकट्ठे देखने चलें । Let us / Let’s go to see a movie together.

Let us / Let’s put our differences aside and make a new start. आओ अपने मतभेद एक तरफ करें और नई शुरुआत करें ।

Let us / Let’s wait until tomorrow. हम कल तक का इंतजार करें ।

Let’s change the subject now. अब हम विषय बदलें ।

Let us / Let’s talk about your son’s engagement. आओ तुम्हारे बेटे की सगाई की बात करें ।

Come/Come on, let us / let’s enquire about his health. चलो, हम उसकी सेहत के बारे में पूछें ।

Let’s not involve in his quarrel. हम उस झगड़े में शामिल ना हो ।

Let’s not walk on the road. हम रोड़ पर ना चलें ।

 Pretty adverb   /ˈprɪt̬.i/  (quite, but not extremely) काफ़ी  1. The house has four bedrooms, so it’s pretty big. 2. I’m pretty sure it was her.     3. It’s pretty noisy at night-time. 4 I found his argument pretty convincing.

Pretty adjective /ˈprɪt̬.i/ (pleasant to look at, or (especially of girls or women or things relating to them) attractive or pleasant) सुंदर, आकर्षक 1. That’s a pretty hat you’re wearing. 2. She’s got such a pretty daughter. 3. She is by no means pretty. 4 a pretty view. (lesson-64)
