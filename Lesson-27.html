Squeak verb /skwiːk/ किकियान /चूं-चूं की ध्वनि to make a short, very high cry or sound: 1-The mice in the cupboard squeaked. 2- The door squeaked as it swung back and forth on its rusty hinges.

meritorious adjective (/ˌmer.əˈtɔːr.i.əs/) सराहनीय. Deserving great praise. 1-We praised him for his meritorious service. 2-Scala was a meritorious public servant.

Casanova     noun ( /ˌkæs.əˈnoʊ.və/) आशिक मिजाज a man who has had a lot of sexual relationships

Ransom noun (/ˈræn.səm/). फिरौती. A large amount of money that a person demands in exchange for someone who is in his prisoner. Or sometimes for an animal: (They demanded a huge ransom for the return of the little girl whom they had kidnapped.)
